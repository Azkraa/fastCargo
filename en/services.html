OUR SERVICES

Providing the best logistics solutions for your needs.

LOGISTICS

Land Freight

Our land freight service is designed to provide flexibility and wide coverage throughout Indonesia. We offer delivery between cities and provinces with modern trucks and experienced drivers. Suitable for various types of goods, from small packages to large shipments. With real-time tracking system, you can monitor the position of your goods anytime. This service also supports door-to-door delivery, ensuring your goods arrive directly at the destination address without hassle.

Sea Freight

Sea freight is an effective solution for large or heavy shipments that require cost efficiency. We collaborate with trusted shipping lines to ensure the safety and punctuality of inter-island deliveries. This service is ideal for manufacturing, trade, and large-scale logistics operations requiring mass shipment. We provide specialized packaging and complete shipping documentation to keep your goods safe during the sea journey.

Air Freight

For urgent shipments that require fast delivery, our air freight service is the perfect choice. We partner with leading airlines to provide reliable and timely delivery services both domestically and internationally. This service is suitable for perishable goods, important documents, and high-value items that need to reach their destination quickly. We also offer customs clearance assistance to ensure a smooth shipping process without delays.

Warehouse

Our warehouse service provides secure and organized storage space for various types of goods. Our facilities are equipped with 24-hour security systems, temperature monitoring for sensitive items, and advanced inventory management. Suitable for both short-term and long-term storage, our warehouse services support faster and more efficient distribution. We also offer handling and repackaging services according to your needs, ensuring your goods are ready to ship at any time.
